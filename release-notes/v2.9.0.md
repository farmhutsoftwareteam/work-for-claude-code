# Atelier 2.9.0 — Observer mode

- **Watch any session live, wherever it runs.** Start a session from a
  terminal, another machine, or any tool other than Atelier — it now
  shows up in the history rail with a pulsing "live" badge. Open it and
  Atelier tails the transcript in real time: the same live chat view,
  background-task strip, and delegation cards you get from a session you
  started here, fully read-only. No composer, no restart, no way to
  accidentally take over a conversation something else owns.
- **Chrome-style tab strip.** Tabs now shrink to fit as you open more of
  them instead of a fixed width that just scrolls off-screen. Once
  they've shrunk as far as they can, a searchable dropdown takes over so
  you can always find a tab by typing part of its title.
- **Fixed: ⌘1–⌘9 and ⌘]/⌘[ didn't do anything.** Jump-to-tab and
  next/previous-tab shortcuts were silently broken — they're wired
  correctly now.
