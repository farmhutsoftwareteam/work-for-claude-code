# Atelier 2.6.4 — your tabs come back

- **Reopening Atelier restores your workspace.** Every tab you had open
  comes back exactly where you left it — conversation on screen, resting
  quietly. Nothing heavy happens at launch: a restored tab uses zero
  resources until you send your next message, which wakes it instantly with
  its full history.
- **Pasting a skills repo link now finds every skill in it.** Repos like
  anthropics/skills bundle many skills in nested folders — Atelier now scans
  the whole repo and shows you a picker when there's more than one, instead
  of finding nothing (or the wrong thing).
- **The duplicate-skills "clean up" button now confirms visibly** — "cleaned
  up N ✓" the moment you click, instead of appearing to do nothing.
