# Atelier 2.5.3 — the transcript stops fighting your scroll

- **Scroll up during a streaming reply and the view now stays put.** Before,
  auto-scroll yanked you back to the bottom ~30 times a second while the
  agent worked — reading anything mid-turn was impossible. The transcript now
  tracks whether you're at the bottom: pinned → it follows the stream;
  scrolled up → it holds perfectly still; scroll back down → tracking resumes
  on its own.
- **Sending a message always snaps to your reply** — that intent is
  unambiguous. The agent's own tool activity can never steal your scroll
  position again.
- **Resumed sessions open at the latest message** instead of landing
  mid-history.
