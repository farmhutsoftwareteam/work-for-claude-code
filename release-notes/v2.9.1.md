# Atelier 2.9.1 — smooth streaming again

- **Fixed: sloppy scroll and content flickering while a reply streams.**
  A renderer change in 2.8.2 (the one that made cross-paragraph text
  selection work) was quietly rebuilding the *entire* message on every
  streaming update — thirty times a second — instead of just the new
  words. Long replies jumped, blanked, and got progressively worse the
  longer they ran. The streaming message now updates only its growing
  tail; cross-paragraph selection still works exactly as before once a
  reply lands.
- **Fixed: rate-limit retries were silently dropped.** Two decode bugs
  meant real 429 retries never showed the "retrying…" indicator, and a
  separate rate-limit status event was being logged and discarded
  entirely instead of parsed. Both are fixed and verified against real
  captured wire data from a live rate-limited session.
