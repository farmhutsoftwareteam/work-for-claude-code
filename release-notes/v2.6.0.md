# Atelier 2.6.0 — Skills, finally reachable

Skills management was quietly working under the hood the whole time — the
"Skills" tile in the left rail just had nowhere to send you. Now it does.

## Skills panel

- **Click "Skills" and get a real panel** — your skills grouped by personal,
  project, and plugin, with live enable/disable, edit, and delete (moved to
  Trash, always undoable).
- **Full editing** — name, description, when-to-use, model, effort, license,
  argument hints, invocation toggles, and the SKILL.md body itself, all in
  one sheet.

## Let Claude write it for you

- **Describe what you want in plain language** and Claude drafts a complete,
  real SKILL.md — not a blank template. Review it before anything saves;
  cancel and nothing touches your skills folder.

## Marketplace

- **Browse skills across every plugin marketplace you have registered** —
  install a single skill without installing its whole plugin.
- **Add a skill from any git repo** — Atelier clones it, finds the SKILL.md,
  and shows you what it is before installing.
