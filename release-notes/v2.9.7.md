# Atelier 2.9.7 — connect Supabase without typing a project ref

- **Supabase now has a real "connect & choose project" flow.** Sign in
  once, then pick your project from an actual list instead of hunting
  down and typing a `project_ref` by hand. Atelier writes a properly
  scoped, token-free config entry for you, with a read-only toggle for
  the cautious.
- **MCP servers can now be deleted and edited in place** from the v2
  panel — no more delete-and-recreate just to fix a wrong credential.
